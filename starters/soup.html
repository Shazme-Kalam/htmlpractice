Chicken Corn Soup RECIPE

Chicken Corn Soup ek bohat hi tasty aur easy ban’nay wala soup hai 👍. Ye khas kar winters me bohat pasand kiya jata hai.

Ingredients (اجزاء)

Chicken (boneless, chhoti pieces ya shredded) – 1 cup

Corn (sweet corn, crushed ya blended) – 1 cup

Chicken stock / pani – 4 cups

Anday – 2 (light beat kar lein)

Corn flour – 3 tbsp (thoda pani mila ke slurry bana lein)

Namak – taste ke mutabiq

Kali mirch – 1 tsp

Soy sauce – 2 tbsp

Vinegar – 2 tbsp

Green chili sauce – 1 tbsp (optional)

Method (ترکیب)

Chicken boil karein – Chicken ko pani aur thoda namak dal ke boil karein. Jab chicken soft ho jaye to usay shred kar lein aur pani ko stock ke liye use karein. Corn add karein – Stock me corn dal kar 5–7 min boil karein. Chicken mix karein – Ab shredded chicken dal dein aur mix karein. Cornflour slurry – Cornflour ka mixture dheere dheere add karein aur continuously stir karte rahein taki lumps na ban’n. Soup slightly thick ho jaye. Egg stream – Beaten eggs ko halka halka soup me dalte jayein aur stir karein (taake threads jese strips ban jayein). Seasoning – Namak, kali mirch adjust karein. Serve – Soup ko garam garam soy sauce, vinegar aur chili sauce ke saath serve karein.

Hot & Sour Sour Recipe

Yeh rahi Hot & Sour Soup ki simple aur tasty recipe jo ghar par easily ban sakti hai:

🥣 Ingredients:

Chicken (boneless, thin strips) – ½ cup

Chicken stock / Yakhni – 4 cups

Carrot (julienne cut) – ½ cup

Cabbage (shredded) – ½ cup

Capsicum (julienne cut) – ½ cup

Cornflour – 3 tbsp (pani me ghola hua)

Soya sauce – 2 tbsp

Vinegar – 2 tbsp

Chili sauce – 1 tbsp

Crushed red chili – 1 tsp (ya taste ke mutabiq)

Black pepper – ½ tsp

Salt – taste ke mutabiq

Egg – 1 (beaten)

Oil – 1 tbsp

🔥 Method:

Ek pan me oil garam kar ke chicken strips daal kar halka sa fry kar lo. Ab isme chicken stock daal do aur boil aane do. Carrot, cabbage aur capsicum daal kar 3-4 min cook karo (zyada overcook na ho). Soya sauce, vinegar, chili sauce, red chili aur black pepper mix kar do. Cornflour paste dheere dheere daal kar continuously stir karo jab tak soup thoda thick na ho jaye. Ab beaten egg ko patli stream me daalo aur stir karte raho taake egg flower ban jaye. Salt adjust karo aur garma garam serve karo.

🍽️ Serving Suggestion:

Isay chili sauce aur soya sauce ke sath serve karo. Upar se halka sa green onion bhi garnish kar sakte ho.
